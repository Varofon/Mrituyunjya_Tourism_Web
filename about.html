ABOUT US
Our Core Vision and Mission


Vision


Our vision is to most sincerely contribute our every effort towards the reestablishment of Sanatana Dharma and resurrect the Vedas in the society and in the world at large. “Grihe Grihe Ved” (Vedas in every Home) is the solemn motto of our Gurudev, Bramharishi Mahamrityunjya Shri Shri Bhrigu Giri Maharaj…

Mission


We are here to help people recreate and strengthen a link with their spiritual self. To achieve this we have formed Mrityunjya Tourism as an effective mechanism to inspire and prepare people with the power of Spiritual Science .Vigorous and potent ideas can change attitudes and lives. Thus we at Mrityunjya Tourism will always endeavor to impact people positively through the bonafide and established principles of Indian culture and wisdom.

Learn Who We Are...

We specialize in spiritual journeys and sacred sites tours to some of the most beautiful and soul-stirring landscapes in Assam. With carefully crafted itineraries and passionate tour leaders you can be sure your sacred travel experience will exceed your every expectation and will help you create a lifetime of memories. At Mrityunjya Tourism we facilitate the learning of many ancient spiritual traditions through their wisdom teachings, ceremonies, and healing practices. Join us on one of our spiritual journeys to connect with the ancient energies that exist. Our tour leaders are ready to share their extensive knowledge, unique perspective, and Vedic wisdom to ensure your spiritual travel experience is both transformative and enjoyable. We take care of all the details so you can leave your worries at home and travel in safety and comfort with a small group of like-minded travellers, the kind of kindred spirits that help forge life-long friendships. Our mission in Mrityunjya Tourism is to improve the quality of life for all beings in our local community through a variety of projects addressing social, educational, environmental and economic issues from a practical, spiritual perspective. We are committed to working with people from all walks of life to find innovative solutions to problems big and small.
